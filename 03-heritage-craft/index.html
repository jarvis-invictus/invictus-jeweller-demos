Three Generations of Golden Legacy

It started with a small workshop in the heart of Narayan Peth. Shri Raghunath Deshpande — our founder — believed that every piece of jewellery should carry a story. Not just gold and gemstones, but the prayers of a mother, the dreams of a bride, the pride of a family.

Sixty years later, his grandson still sits at the same workbench, shaping naths by hand the way Aajoba taught him. We've grown — yes — but the philosophy hasn't changed: honest gold, fair making charges, and the kind of personal attention that makes you feel like family, not a customer.

Every piece that leaves our store is BIS hallmarked, handchecked, and blessed. Because in Pune, your jeweller isn't just a shopkeeper — he's part of your family's milestones.
