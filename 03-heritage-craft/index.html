Our Story

54 Years Rooted in
Maharashtra's Heart

In 1972, Dattatray Mangale opened a small jewellery shop in Pune's old city with a simple promise — pure gold, honest prices, and jewellery that honours tradition. Over five decades, that promise has never changed.

Today, Mangal Jewellers is a name trusted by three generations of Pune families. Our pieces are worn at every auspicious occasion — from a daughter's first ear piercing to a grandmother's 50th anniversary.

"Gold is not just metal — it is blessings passed from mother to daughter, from generation to generation."

We specialise in jewellery that carries Maharashtra's soul — Kolhapuri saaj, temple motifs, traditional nath. Crafted by artisans who learned from their fathers, who learned from their fathers.
