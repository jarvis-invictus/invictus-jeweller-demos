What We Work With

Materials & Gemstones

We source ethically and work only with materials we'd trust on our own hands. Every stone comes certified, every alloy tested.

🥇

14K Gold

Durable everyday wear. Perfect for rings and bracelets that won't leave your wrist. Available in yellow, rose, and white.

✦

18K Gold

Our most popular choice. Rich colour, excellent durability — the sweet spot for engagement rings and statement pieces.

👑

22K Gold

Traditional Indian purity. Deep, warm lustre that's unmistakable. Best for pendants, earrings, and heritage-inspired designs.

🪙

Sterling Silver

Contemporary and versatile. Great for men's jewellery, everyday pendants, and budget-friendly custom designs.

💎

Lab Diamonds

Identical to mined — at a fraction of the cost and zero ethical compromise. IGI certified. Available up to 3 carats.

🌿

Natural Gemstones

Emeralds, sapphires, rubies, tourmalines — hand-selected from trusted Jaipur and Sri Lankan sources. Every stone comes with provenance.

Our Story

A Workshop, Not a Showroom

"I wanted to build the kind of jeweller I could never find — one that actually listens."

VeraForm Studio started in 2018 in a small rented workshop in Aundh, Pune. Meera Deshpande had just left her product design job at a Bangalore startup, armed with six months of traditional jewellery training in Rajasthan and a conviction that young Indians deserved better than mass-produced gold sets.

The first commission was an engagement ring for a college friend — a low-profile setting with a family heirloom diamond reset in brushed rose gold. No traditional jeweller would touch it. Meera made it in eleven days. Word spread.

By 2020, the workshop moved to Koregaon Park. Today, VeraForm is a team of four — Meera, two master karigars with over 40 years of combined experience, and a 3D design specialist. We've handcrafted over 300 bespoke pieces for clients across Pune, Mumbai, and Bangalore.

We still don't have a display counter. Every piece in our studio was made for someone specific — and we think that's exactly how it should be.

Meera Deshpande
Founder & Lead Designer
