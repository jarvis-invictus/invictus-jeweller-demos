Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Aurum Lab

What are lab-grown diamonds at Aurum Lab Pune? +
Lab-grown diamonds are real diamonds created using CVD/HPHT technology with identical chemical, physical, and optical properties to mined diamonds. 40-70% more affordable and 100% conflict-free. All IGI/GIA certified.
Are Aurum Lab diamonds certified and genuine? +
Yes, every Aurum Lab diamond comes with IGI or GIA certification — the world's most trusted gemological labs. Each stone has a laser inscription and full 4C grading report. Same standards as mined diamonds.
How much cheaper are lab-grown diamonds at Aurum Lab? +
Lab-grown diamonds are 40-70% less expensive. A 1-carat VS1 E lab diamond costs ~₹1,45,000 vs ~₹4,20,000 mined. Spend the savings on a better cut, larger stone, or premium setting.
Does Aurum Lab create custom engagement rings? +
Yes, we specialise in custom engagement rings. Choose your lab-grown diamond, design your setting with 3D CAD renders, and our craftsmen create your perfect ring in 7-14 working days in 18K/22K gold or platinum.
