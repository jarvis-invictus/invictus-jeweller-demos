The Science

Same Diamond. Smarter Choice.

Lab-grown diamonds aren't imitations. They're real diamonds — created in a controlled environment instead of a mine. Same carbon atoms, same crystal structure, same sparkle. The only difference is origin.

Trust, Verified

Every Piece is Certified. No Exceptions.

We don't sell uncertified stones. If it doesn't have a grading report from a recognized lab, it doesn't leave our counter.

IGI

International Gemological Institute

The world's largest independent gem certification lab. Every diamond we sell above 0.3 carats carries an IGI report grading the 4Cs — cut, clarity, colour, and carat weight. Laser-inscribed on the girdle for traceability.

GIA

Gemological Institute of America

The gold standard in diamond grading since 1931. They literally invented the 4C system. Select pieces in our premium collection carry GIA reports — the same certification Tiffany & Co. uses.

BIS

BIS Hallmark (Bureau of Indian Standards)

All our gold jewellery is BIS Hallmarked — that's the Indian government's guarantee that the gold purity is exactly what we say it is. 14K means 14K. 18K means 18K. No trust issues.
