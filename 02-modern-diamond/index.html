  • ◆
    100% BIS Hallmarked Gold
    Every gold piece carries BIS Hallmark — the government's purity guarantee. 22KT (916) and 18KT certified. You always know exactly what you're buying.
  • ◆
    IGI Certified Diamonds Only
    We only sell IGI-certified diamonds with full grading reports. Colour, clarity, cut, carat — all transparently disclosed. No verbal assurances.
  • ◆
    Transparent Making Charges
    Fixed making charges displayed on every tag. No surprise deductions at billing. What you see is what you pay — always.
  • ◆
    Lifetime Exchange Policy
    Exchange any Ornate piece at its current gold rate, any time. No time limit. Upgrade your collection as your style evolves.
  • ◆
    In-House Gemologist
    GIA-trained gemologist on staff. All diamonds graded in-house before purchase. Valuation certificates issued on request.
